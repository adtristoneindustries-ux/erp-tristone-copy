## Access

### Staff:
- Login as staff
- Click "Digital Classroom" in sidebar
- Upload materials with class/section selection

### Student:
- Login as student
- Click "Digital Classroom" in sidebar
- View materials by subject
- Click "View All Materials" for full list

## Features

### Staff Can:
- Upload Video/Image/PDF/Document files (up to 100MB)
- Add URL links (YouTube, Google Drive, etc.)
- Select specific class and section
- Choose subject for material
- Delete uploaded materials
- View all their uploaded materials

### Students Can:
- See subject cards with material preview
- View first 2 videos per subject
- Open modal to see all materials
- Watch videos, view images, download files
- Get real-time updates when staff uploads
